In the early period, the Danes have their best relative starting position. They have territory nearby in Scandinavia that can be conquered quickly and is relatively easy to defend—Denmark is the only land route to the north.

在游戏的早期阶段，丹麦人拥有相对最佳的起始位置。他们在斯堪的纳维亚附近有领土，可以迅速征服且相对容易防守——丹麦是通往北方的唯一陆路。

The Danes also have exclusive access to Vikings and Longboats. While these will become outmoded over time, for the first years of play, they are simply better than forces available to other factions. This advantage may be short-lived, but they are vital to an early, aggressive policy and give the ability to seize chances.

丹麦人还独享维京人和长船的使用权。虽然这些在未来会过时，但在游戏的最初几年，它们绝对优于其他势力可用的部队。这种优势可能是短暂的，但对早期的进攻政策至关重要，使他们能够抓住机会。

The lands of the north are uncompromising, and they have always produced superlative fighting men. The Vikings, the Normans, the Rus in Novgorod and Moscow, and even the Varangian Guard in far-off Constantinople are all Norsemen of one sort or another.

北方的土地不妥协，始终产生卓越的战士。维京人、诺曼人、诺夫哥罗德和莫斯科的罗斯，甚至远在君士坦丁堡的瓦兰吉卫队，都是不同类型的诺斯人。

As navigators, traders, and warriors, Norsemen have few rivals in the world, although their raiding voyages are now nearly at an end. Denmark is now established as the strongest of the Scandinavian kingdoms, having crushed the pagan Wends in northern Germany and their own pagans in the wilder lands to the north.

作为航海者、商人和战士，诺斯人在世界上几乎没有对手，尽管他们的掠夺航行现在几乎已结束。丹麦现已成为斯堪的纳维亚王国中最强大的国家，击败了北德的异教温德人和北方更野蛮的异教徒。

The Danes can do well if they can avoid being overrun by Imperial forces outside Scandinavia. They have a reasonably good chance of securing a mini-empire from Scandinavian and Baltic coastal provinces.

如果丹麦人能够避免被斯堪的纳维亚以外的帝国军队压倒，他们就能做得很好。他们有相当好的机会从斯堪的纳维亚和波罗的海沿岸省份建立一个迷你帝国。

Denmark is the only land bridge into Scandinavia, making the defense of new possessions relatively straightforward. Their position at the mouth of the Baltic makes development as a maritime power an attractive, and possibly vital, option.

丹麦是进入斯堪的纳维亚的唯一陆桥，这使得新领土的防御相对简单。他们在波罗的海入口的位置使得发展为海洋强国成为一个有吸引力的，甚至可能是至关重要的选择。

If the Baltic can be controlled, then Danish armies can move at will to any point along the coast. The independent duchies and principalities can be picked off and then used to expand Danish power further along the northern periphery of Europe.

如果能够控制波罗的海，丹麦军队就可以自由地在沿海的任何地方移动。独立的公国和小国可以逐个击破，然后用来进一步扩展丹麦在欧洲北部边缘的力量。

This policy can bring a Danish ruler into competition with Poland, Novgorod, and the Holy Roman Empire, as these may be trying to acquire the same lands on the Baltic coast.

这一政策可能使丹麦统治者与波兰、诺夫哥罗德和神圣罗马帝国竞争，因为这些国家可能会试图获取波罗的海沿岸的同样土地。

Southern expansion into the rich provinces of central and western Germany is also an option for a strong Danish ruler, but one that must be carefully timed to avoid an Imperial backlash. It is a strategy that works well when Imperial authority has collapsed.

向中部和西部德国富饶省份的南部扩张也是强大丹麦统治者的一个选项，但必须仔细把握时机，以避免帝国的反击。当帝国权威崩溃时，这一策略效果显著。

Finally, it is less than a century ago that Danish rulers could also claim the kingship of England, before the coming of the Norman overlords (descendants of Vikings themselves) who now rule there.

最后，不到一个世纪前，丹麦统治者也曾声称拥有英格兰的王位，直到诺曼霸主（他们自己也是维京人的后裔）统治了那里。

Perhaps these old claims could be renewed, and a new maritime kingdom across the Northern seas could be created. Wherever a ship can go there could be a way for the Danish to make a profit - or a killing!

或许这些旧的权利可以被重申，一个新的横跨北海的海洋王国可以被建立。只要有船能够到达的地方，丹麦人就能找到盈利的机会——甚至是大获其利！
