Hemmed in by other factions, the Italians need to choose their enemies with care. While the lands of the Papacy may seem like tempting targets on the way to uniting the Italian peninsula, the unique status of the Papacy itself makes it an almost-impossible opponent to completely eradicate.

被其他势力包围，意大利人需要小心选择敌人。虽然教皇的土地在统一意大利半岛的过程中似乎是诱人的目标，但教皇本身的独特地位使其成为几乎不可能完全消灭的对手。

The Italians have only one exclusive unit, Italian Light Infantry, but they are one of the two factions that can eventually train Gothic Knights and Gothic Sergeants - among the most effective units available to western Christian factions. They also have the potential to train superior handgunners and arquebusiers, and in Tuscany at least to train Urban Militia of a higher than average standard.

意大利人只有一个专属单位：意大利轻步兵，但他们是能够最终训练哥特骑士和哥特军士的两个势力之一——这些单位在西方基督教势力中非常有效。他们还有潜力训练更优秀的火枪手和火绳枪手，至少在托斯卡纳地区可以训练出高于平均水平的城市民兵。

The Italians are blessed with rich lands, industry, and wealth from trade besides. Much of Europe's wealth flows into Italy and the coffers of the Pope, and much of his wealth then flows out into the hands of Italian merchants.

意大利人拥有富饶的土地、工业以及来自贸易的财富。欧洲的大部分财富流入意大利和教皇的金库，教皇的财富又流向意大利商人手中。

All of this gives Italy's rulers a chance to become great, for as the Roman writer Cicero put it: "nervos belli, pecuniam infinitam" (the sinews of war, unlimited money). They are also blessed with a warlike and adventurous nature, which can make them a difficult people to rule at times.

所有这些都给意大利的统治者提供了成就伟大的机会，正如罗马作家西塞罗所说：“nervos belli, pecuniam infinitam”（战争的筋脉，无限的金钱）。他们也天生具有好斗和冒险的性格，这使得他们在某些时候成为难以统治的人民。

Opportunities come in many guises for a bold Italian ruler. To the northwest, the French hold on their southern provinces is not always as secure as the northern French kings would wish; religious and secular differences can mean that France has problems with its own people even while it is struggling against English expansionism.

对于一位大胆的意大利统治者来说，机会有多种表现形式。在西北方向，法国对其南部省份的控制并不总是像北方的法国国王所希望的那样稳固；宗教和世俗的差异可能意味着法国在与英格兰扩张斗争的同时，也与本国人民产生问题。

To the north, Imperial power can slip or fail. To the east, the Balkans can be a tempting potential empire for Italy. And there is always the prospect of becoming a naval power. Perhaps, in the long term, domination of the Mediterranean offers the best chance of Italian supremacy.

在北方，帝国的力量可能会滑落或失败。在东面，巴尔干可以成为意大利一个诱人的潜在帝国。而且总是有成为海洋强国的可能性。或许，从长远来看，统治地中海提供了意大利达到至高无上的最佳机会。

True, the great powers of the Byzantine Empire and the Islamic kingdoms will have to be challenged for naval and mercantile supremacy. With the wealth of Mediterranean trade at his disposal and his home provinces secured, any Italian ruler should be able to expand his holdings.

诚然，拜占庭帝国和伊斯兰王国的大国将不得不挑战海洋和商业的霸权。凭借地中海贸易的财富和稳固的家乡省份，任何意大利统治者都应该能够扩展其领地。

All of these strategies have their risks. The same wealth that helps Italy also makes it an attractive target to others. Both the French King and the Emperor may not be able to resist meddling in Italy, or even mounting a full-scale invasion.

所有这些策略都有其风险。帮助意大利的财富也使其成为其他势力的诱人目标。法国国王和皇帝都可能无法抵制干预意大利，甚至发动全面入侵的诱惑。

Competing at sea means taking power that the Byzantines may consider to be rightfully theirs, or may weaken the Byzantines so that they no longer form a bulwark against future Islamic expansion. But if Italy benefits from all these machinations and from a Crusade or two...

在海上竞争意味着夺取拜占庭人可能认为理应属于他们的权力，或者可能削弱拜占庭，使其不再成为抵御未来伊斯兰扩张的堡垒。但是，如果意大利能从所有这些阴谋和几次十字军东征中获益……
