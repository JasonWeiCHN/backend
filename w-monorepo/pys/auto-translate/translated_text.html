Chapter 1 I HATE HAVING TO dress like a man.

第1章我讨厌打扮得像男人一样。

The cotton shirt is too loose, the breeches too big, the boots too uncomfortable.

棉衬衫太松了，马裤太大，靴子太不舒服了。

My hair is bound on the top of my head, secured in a bun underneath a small sailor’s hat.

我的头发被束缚在我的头顶上，固定在小水手帽子下面的面包上。

My sword is strapped tightly to the left side of my waist, a pistol undrawn on my right.

我的剑被紧紧绑在腰的左侧，右边的手枪没有掉。

The clothing is awkward as it hangs loose in all the wrong places.

衣服很尴尬，因为它在所有错误的地方都悬挂着。

And the smell!

和气味！

You’d think men did nothing but roll around in dead fish guts while smearing their own excrement on their sleeves.

您会认为男人只能在死鱼肠道上滚来滚去时，一边涂在袖子上的粪便。

But perhaps I shouldn’t complain so.

但是也许我不应该这样抱怨。

Such precautions are necessary when one’s being invaded by pirates.

当一个人被海盗入侵时，必须采取这种预防措施。

We’re outnumbered.

我们的人数超过了。

Outgunned.

超过。

Seven of my men lie dead on their backs.

我的七个人躺在他们的背上。

Two more jumped overboard as soon as they saw the black flag of the Night Farer on the horizon.

当他们看到地平线上的夜晚的黑旗时，另外两个人跳下了船。

Deserters.

逃兵。

They’re the most cowardly filth.

它们是最胆小的肮脏。

They deserve whatever fate comes to them.

他们应该得到任何命运。

Whether they tire and drown or get claimed by the sea life.

无论他们是疲倦，淹死还是被海洋生物索取。

Steel twangs through the air.

钢twang在空中。

The ship rocks from the blasts of cannons.

该船从大炮的爆炸中摇摆。

We cannot hold out much longer.

我们不能坚持更长的时间。

“Two more down, Captain,” Mandsy, my temporary first mate, says from where she peeks through the trapdoor.

“又有两个，船长，”我的临时第一伴侣曼迪从陷阱门偷看的地方说。

“I should be up there, shoving steel between ribs,” I say, “not hiding like some helpless whelp.” “A little patience,” she reminds me.

我说：“我应该在那儿，在肋骨之间推过钢铁，”我说，“不像一些无助的鞭子那样躲藏起来。”“有点耐心，”她提醒我。

“If we’re to survive this, you need to stay put.” “Survive?” I ask, offended.

“如果我们要生存这个，您需要留下来。”“存活？”我问，冒犯了。

“Let me rephrase.

“让我改写。

If we’re to succeed , you really shouldn’t be seen performing impressive feats with the sword.” “But maybe if I just killed a few of them…” I say more to myself.

如果我们要取得成功，那么您真的不应该看到剑表演令人印象深刻的壮举。”“但是，也许如果我只是杀死了其中的一些……”我对自己说更多。

“You know we can’t risk that,” she says.

她说：“你知道我们不能冒险。”

Then she adds abruptly, “More men have boarded the ship.

然后她突然补充说：“越来越多的人登上了这艘船。

I think they’re headed this way.”

我认为他们是这样的。”
